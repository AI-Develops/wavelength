The Circle of Fifths

A visual representation of the relationships between the 12 tones of the chromatic scale. Each step clockwise moves up a perfect fifth, while counterclockwise moves down a fifth. This elegant diagram reveals key signatures, relative majors and minors, and harmonic connections.

Piano & Scales

Explore different musical scales on the piano keyboard. Select a root note and scale type to visualize the pattern. Each scale creates a unique mood and character in music.

Chord Builder

Build and explore chord structures. Understanding chords is fundamental to harmony. Select a root note and chord type to see and hear the notes that create different harmonic colors.
